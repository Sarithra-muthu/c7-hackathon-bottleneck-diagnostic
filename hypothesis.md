# Move 2: Hypothesis

**The claim:**
A stranger handed my one diagnostic sentence reacts with recognition and goes on to act on it, more often than they give it a polite nod and stay where they were.

**Result that proves me right:**
2 out of 2 people show visible recognition (a reaction, not just words) AND take a real next step because of the sentence.

**Result that means no problem exists:**
2 out of 2 people just politely agree with no visible reaction or behavioral change.

**Kill-number:**
If 0 out of 2 people show any behavioral change after hearing the sentence, I accept my tool only describes , it doesn't diagnose.
